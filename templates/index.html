Smart License Plate Recognition

Our AI-powered LPR system accurately and quickly recognizes Vietnamese vehicle license plates using high-resolution imaging and advanced deep learning algorithms. This solution supports parking efficiency by facilitating faster entry and exit processes, reducing wait times, and minimizing human intervention.

How to Use

1. Click "Choose File" and select an image of the license plate
2. Click "Recognize" to process
3. View the detected plate number and processing time

The system will display the image with a bounding box around the detected license plate.

Requirements

• Image resolution: 1920×1080 (Full HD) recommended
• License plate: At least 5% of image area
• Visibility: Clear, not obscured or overexposed
• Rotation: In-plane rotation ≤ 30 degrees
• Format: Standard Vietnamese vehicle plates
