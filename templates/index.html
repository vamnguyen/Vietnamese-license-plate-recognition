We develop a Vietnamese license plate recognition model for smart parking systems

The goal is to create an LPR system that accurately and quickly recognizes vehicle license plates using high-resolution imaging and advanced algorithms. This solution supports parking efficiency by facilitating faster entry and exit processes, reducing wait times, and minimizing human intervention.

How to use

1. Click "Choose File" button, select the image of the license plate you want to recognize
2. Upload the image.
3. Click the "Upload and Recognize" button to process the recognition.
You will receive the license plate content and processing time.
Additionally, the website will display the image with a bounding box around the license plate.

Constraints

1. The image resolution is 1920x1080 (Full HD).
2. The license plate occupies at least 5% of the image area.
3. The license plate is clearly visible, not obscured, not overexposed, not poorly lit, and not deformed.
4. The image has in-plane rotations of the license plate no greater than 30 degrees along the x-axis
5. The license plate follows the standard format of Vietnamese vehicle plates.
